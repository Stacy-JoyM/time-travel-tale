

Future 
Ava stood at the edge of the shimmering skyline, the city stretching out before her like a gleaming mosaic of glass and steel.
It was a world she barely recognized, where soaring skyscrapers pierced through the clouds and vehicles glided silently above the 
streets, powered by technology that defied her understanding. The air buzzed with a harmonious hum of advanced machinery, and holographic displays floated seamlessly alongside towering digital billboards.
